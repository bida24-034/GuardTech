Policy

Our Policy

Claiming Period
01

3-Month Claim Waiting Period

Once a claim has been successfully processed, you must wait 3 calendar months before submitting another claim. The waiting period starts from the date your claim is approved.

Payment
02

Non-Payment Removal

If a subscriber fails to pay for 3 consecutive months, their plan is automatically cancelled and they are removed from coverage. A new registration will be required to re-join.

Benefit Tiers
03

Standard vs Premium — Beyond Repair

Standard plans cover repair costs only. If your device is assessed as beyond repair, GuardTech is not liable for replacement under a Standard plan.

Premium plans include beyond-repair cover — if your device cannot be fixed, we will work with you toward a replacement solution.

General Terms All plans are subject to assessment at drop-off. GuardTech reserves the right to decline repairs where damage is assessed as intentional or outside the scope of the selected plan. Coverage begins from the date of first successful payment. GuardTech is based at BAC (also known as BSBS) in Gaborone. Our service is open to all university students in Gaborone — you do not need to be a BAC student to register.

Our Story

Protection Born On Campus

We understand student life in Gaborone. A broken device shouldn't derail your academic journey — no matter which university you attend.

Mission

Why We Built GuardTech

We started GuardTech because we saw students losing valuable study time — and money — whenever their devices broke. Repair shops were expensive, far from campus, and not built with students in mind.

GuardTech brings the repair service to campus. Drop your device off at BAC (also known as BSBS) in Gaborone. We collect it, take it for repair, and return it back to you on campus. No wasted trips, no hassle.

Our service is open to all university students in Gaborone — UB, BIUST, Limkokwing, Ba Isago, ABM, and more. You don't have to be a BAC student to register and use GuardTech.
